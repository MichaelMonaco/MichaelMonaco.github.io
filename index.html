Ongoing projects

One of my current research projects uses the formalism I developed in my thesis to adapt a result of Livernet, Frisse, and Shnider–Von Osdol about the (co)bar construction for operads to unital, cubical bimodules. I recently gave a talk about this in the talk 1.

In another project, we consider Hopf algebras associated to factorizable bimodules and provide a homological characterization of their associated \(B^+\)-operators in the sense of Connes and Kreimer. I presented this research in talks 2 and 4.
